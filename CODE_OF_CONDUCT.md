# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone who interacts with this repository.  

As this project is primarily a personal collection by **Raphael (@rphlrz)**, contributors and visitors are expected to act with integrity, kindness, and professionalism.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Using respectful and inclusive language
- Providing constructive feedback
- Acknowledging different points of view
- Focusing on learning and collaboration

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Sharing or using content without permission
- Promoting external or commercial content
